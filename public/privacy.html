5. How long we keep it

  • Audit submissions (name, email, answers, score): kept for 24 months from submission, then deleted or fully anonymised.
  • Consent record (timestamp + policy version): kept for 24 months after the last related communication, as the legal record of consent under UK GDPR Art. 7(1).
  • Unsubscribed contacts: retained as a suppression record (email + unsubscribed_at) so we do not accidentally email you again. Deleted on erasure request.
  • Server logs (Netlify): retained according to Netlify's policy (typically 30 days).

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you (Art. 15).
  • Portability — get a machine-readable copy (Art. 20). Use the Download my data link in any email we send you.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data (Art. 16).
  • Erasure — request deletion (Art. 17). Use the Delete my data link in any email we send you, or email us.
  • Restriction and objection (Art. 18 and 21).
  • Withdraw consent at any time (Art. 7(3)). Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
  • Lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

To exercise any of these rights, email marton.gaspar.uk@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies and local storage
